About Reagen Schulenberg

Hi! My name is Reagen Schulenberg. I was born and raised here in Nebraska, so going to the University of Nebraska-Lincoln wasn’t a hard decision for me. I am a double major in Advertising and Public Relations and Sports Media and Communications with a couple minors. I am a member of a sorority here on campus, Kappa Alpha Theta. I also am a volunteer leader for YoungLife, where I lead and mentor a group of high school girls. I really like to adventure and love visiting new places. Traveling is one of my favorite things to do, especially with family and friends!
